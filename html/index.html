About Gwalia Records

Throughout the history of the company, Gwalia Records has collaborated with several leading and popular musicians. In its catalog there are different and strong creative groups. For example, bands such as The Wandering Souls and The River's Edge credited their success to publishing their music through Gwalia Records. Gwalia Records' album performances are usually a sign of high quality music, enthusiasm and extra creativity, which is why the company has gained mass popularity in the field of music work. Keeping this public criticism in mind, Gwalia Records provides a good educational environment among musicians with extensive concerts, tours and music festival participation.
